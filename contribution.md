## Why we require a CLA

We require a Contributor License Agreement (CLA) so the project can be
relicensed in the future without needing to re-contact every contributor.

Current license is AGPL-3.0 to protect the project in its early stage.
As the project matures, we may adopt a more permissive license (for example MIT)
to reduce adoption friction.

Important:

- You keep ownership of your contributions.
- The CLA grants YGGDRASIL-AI rights to use, sublicense, and relicense contributions.
- This is to preserve long-term licensing flexibility and project sustainability.
